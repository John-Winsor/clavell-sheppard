Clavell Sheppard's seven known marriages

Clavell Sheppard was a predator.  A shark swimming in an ocean of desperate women. Women who would find the offer of marriage irresistible.  An apparent solution to their problems of poverty and loneliness, hopefully a guarantee of happiness and security with the added prospect of future childbirth.

Ancestry shows that Clavell Sheppard married seven women.  One marriage was in Alberta, Canada and all the others were in the UK.  Children were born from most or all of these marriages, but this information is restricted by Ancestry's policy of privacy.  Ancestry contains no record of any divorce so Clavell was a serial bigamist as well as a confidence trickster.  This is the behaviour of a psychopath.
